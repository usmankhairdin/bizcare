How It Works

Employer sets a budget

Employers decide how much tax-free money to contribute each month, instead of offering a traditional group health plan.

Employees choose a plan

Employees use the budget to shop for their own individual health insurance (on or off the exchange) that meets their family’s needs.

Employer Pays the Invoice

BizCare generates an exact invoice, and employers pay it.

Enrollments are Submitted

Enrollments are sent to the carriers ahead of the group effective date.
